What I do...

I was born to be an entrpreneur. My whole life I've always found a way to make my own money . I took my hustling to a new level when I started my math tutoring business. I started reading a lot of entrpreneurship books to find out what I needed to do to have a succesful business. During that time was when I figured out I needed a website more than anything else. So I created my own website using Wordpress and I started receiving tons of emails since. Lastly, I learned that if I could code websites from scratch I would make big money and thats where it all started.

First things first, I've been into coding for about three months now. It's really becoming like second nature to me. I can code in languages such as HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Little do you know I wasn't really interested in the tech industry until I found out how much money was behind it. My father tried to get me to start coding years ago which I thought was prettty boring. I've met so many people who know how to code and that is what changed my point of view on programming.
